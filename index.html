Information About Me

Hello, I'm Cindy Su

I'm a recent graduate from Ryerson University's RTA - New Media program with a Bachelor of Fine Arts and a concentration in Computational Media. My time at Ryerson has provided me with a well rounded foundation in digital fabrication, video production, interaction design, experience design, interactive storytelling, gaming and mobile development, tangible interfaces and 3D printing. I have a passion for all things creative and enjoy bringing abstract ideas to life.

I’m a self-motivated and positive individual who is eager to learn about new technologies, new skills, business issues and is open to taking on new challenges. I am able to work well both on my own initiative and as part of a team. I’m looking for opportunities where I can transfer my skills and passion to help companies build their brand and grow their business. I want to work with a supportive team that runs on clear communication. My goal is to align myself with a company I believe in and work alongside with to grow together.
